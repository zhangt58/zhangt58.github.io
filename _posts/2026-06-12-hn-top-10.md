### 10. [AUR Packages Compromised with Infostealer and Rootkit](https://discourse.ifin.network/t/400-aur-packages-compromised-with-infostealer-and-rootkit/577)
⭐ 29 💬 2 👤 keyle
[🔗 Discuss on HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48500447)

Over 400 Arch User Repository packages were compromised in a supply chain attack where a malicious maintainer injected preinstall scripts that downloaded a harmful npm package. This payload delivered an infostealer and an eBPF rootkit to affected systems. Arch Linux users are urged to verify their exposure, rotate all credentials, and consider a full system reinstall due to the severity of the compromise.
