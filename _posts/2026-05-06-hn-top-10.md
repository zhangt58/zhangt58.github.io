### 9. [Accelerating Gemma 4: faster inference with multi-token prediction drafters](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/multi-token-prediction-gemma-4/)
⭐ 585 &nbsp; 💬 273 &nbsp; 👤 amrrs
[🔗 Discuss on HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48024540)

Google is releasing Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) drafters for the Gemma 4 family, enabling up to 3x inference speedup through speculative decoding. The lightweight drafter predicts multiple future tokens at once, which the target model then verifies in parallel — accepting the entire sequence in a single forward pass. This addresses the memory-bandwidth bottleneck in standard LLM inference, delivering dramatically improved responsiveness for on-device AI, chat applications, voice interfaces, and agentic workflows.
